There have been reports of similar incursions by Russian drones into other European countries' airspace throughout the Autumn of 2025. And more recently, Poland's government announced on November 20 that it would be deploying military personnel in response to suspected Russian sabotage attacks on the Warsaw-Lublin rail line near Mika.<br><br>

As a part of this effort, the Polish government is negotiating with its NATO partners to host additional NATO military personnel.
